Economics | Bachelor of Arts | B.A.

The Department of Economics is as old as the college which was established in 1948. The department has been catering to students in the Commerce stream along with the Humanities stream since 1980. The first head of the department was Mr. Gajra followed by Mrs. Maya Mirapuri, Dr. Harver, Mrs. Adarsh Suri and Dr Anuradha Kalhan Siddique. The department is currently headed by Ms Sarita Jai Shankar. The department offers a full majors program in the final year to the cream of students enrolled in the BA program. The curriculum of each course has been designed to enhance the studentsâ€™ theoretical and practical knowledge of the subject and to equip them for both further specialization in the field and employment. Our students have been securing admission at prestigious institutions like London School of Economics and Political Science, Harvard, University of Warwick, Barcelona Graduate School of Economics, Imperial, LBS, IIM etc to pursue further studies in Economics, Finance, and Management. On the placements front, our students have been picked up by companies like Google, RBI, Ernst & Young, KPMG, JLL, Edelweiss, Tres Vista, Zomato, Crisil, Royal Bank of Scotland and successful NGOs like Teach for India from campus and have thus made entry into the diverse fields of finance, research, social work, banking, start-ups, teaching, and civil services at the professional level.
Departmental strength also consists of six assistant professors, among them four are the Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) specialized in various areas of economics. Our department strives to reach standard of excellence in teaching and research activities. Teaching faculties of the department allocate projects and guide on different economic related issues. Department offers six papers in TYBA including Econometrics and Mathematics which make the course more practical and skill oriented. Our department has special cabin which helps students in group learning.
