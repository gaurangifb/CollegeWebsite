Biotechnology | Bachelor of Science | B.Sc

The subject of Biotechnology was started by the University of Mumbai and Ramniranjan Jhunjhunwala College in 2002-2003. It’s a three year Degree course. There are 35 seats per class and admission is done on H.S.C. Merit basis. It’s an unaided course. The syllabus has been drafted by the faculty to represent major areas of Biotechnology.

Acheivements from 2004 onwards:

The academic year, 2004-2005, opened with College hosting the T.Y. Syllabus Committee workshop for all colleges in Mumbai having Biotechnology at the Degree level. First Batch of B.Sc. Biotechnology graduated in 2004-05. Results have always been 100%. The Biotechnology Association, Mumbai Chapter was established and Dr AN Joshi is vice-chairman.

Achievements of Biotechnology Association-

B.Sc. Biotech students do not need to appear for M.Sc. Biotech entrance examination Admission to M.Sc. Biotech Course is on merit of B. Sc. Biotech marks The Academic audit Committee Inspection took place on the 22nd September 2005. Dr. Tewari, Principal, Maharshi Dayanand College and Dr Vaman Rao, Vice Principal St. Xavier’s College came for this inspection. They were equally pleased with the department, students and faculty. Staff is very active and attends various National and International Conferences/ seminars / workshops to keep themselves abreast with the latest developments and techniques in the field of Biotechnology.
